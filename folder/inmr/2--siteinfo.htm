TECHNICAL SITE VISIT TO WACKER CHEMIE

Lovely Town of Burghausen (Thursday, Oct 22) Join fellow attendees for a unique excursion to visit one of the world’s largest manufacturers of silicone rubber, used across many industries including production of high voltage components such as insulators, arresters and cable accessories.

 

A private bus will take you at 09:00 Thursday from the Westin Grand Munich Hotel to Burghausen, where you will have an exclusive tour of the huge Wacker facility as well as a visit to the Application Engineering Labs of the Silicones Division. This will be followed by Presentation: Wacker Chemie AG in Germany and a light lunch in the cantina. In the afternoon, visitors will be taken on a guided walk through historic Burghausen Castle – the longest in all of Europe.

The bus will return to the Westin Grand Munich (circa 19:00) via Munich Airport (arrival at airport circa 18:00), allowing visitors to conveniently catch flights home anytime after about 19:30.
