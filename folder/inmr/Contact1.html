About INMR

INMR is the world's leading technical journal in the field of electrical insulators, surge arresters, bushings and cable accessories, as well as inspection equipment & laboratory testing services for transmission and distribution applications. Mission: INMR provides you the latest industry technologies and products guide With more than 50,000 readers, We delivery the latest technologies and products news to engineers. INMR is as well known as the independent source portal for insulators, arresters, bushing, and cable accessories.
