About

Streamer provides innovative solutions improving the reliability of medium and high voltage electrical networks.

 

Streamer products ensure protection of equipment against lightning strikes, surges and consequences of such phenomena, as well as help our customers  to reduce the outage’s time due to transient and permanent faults. The solutions are aimed to increase reliability, reduce maintenance procedures and costs as well as to provide industrial safety.

Products

  • Lightning line arresters
  • Fault indicators
